About me

Bio

I am an Invited Assistant Professor at Instituto Superior Técnico - Universidade de Lisboa, researcher at the Distributed Systems Group (GSD) of INESC-ID and a member of DCentral. 

I have a BSc and a MSc in Informatics Engineering from Faculdade de Ciências da Universidade de Lisboa and a PhD in Computer Sciences and Engineering from Instituto Superior Técnico. 

As a researcher I participated in the following projects: PATI, SITAN, SafeClou, SEAL and BIG ERA Chair.

My research interests are related with distributed systems, cybersecurity and cloud. More specifically, I have been researching novel intrusion recovery methods for applications and systems running in the cloud environment.
